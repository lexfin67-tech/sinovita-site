About SinoVita

关于我们

We are a sourcing partner that connects nutraceutical and cosmetic producers directly to vetted Chinese manufacturers — at factory pricing, without the overhead of building a China supply chain from scratch.

Who We Are

Your bridge to Chinese manufacturers

Most buyers who try to source raw materials from China hit the same wall: hundreds of listings, unverified factories, language barriers, MOQ surprises and unpredictable logistics. Building reliable direct relationships takes years.

That's the work we've already done. We maintain direct partnerships with GMP-certified manufacturers across Mainland China for extracts, chelates, amino acids, vitamins, capsules and packaging. You send a product list; we return factory-direct pricing, the manufacturer's documentation, and a single point of contact who handles QC coordination, consolidation, shipping and customs.

We are not a manufacturer. We are the layer that makes buying from manufacturers fast, documented and predictable — and our pricing reflects the factory, not a re-seller's markup.
